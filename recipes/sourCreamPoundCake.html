Sour Cream Pound Cake

Home

Image by wirestock on Freepik

Ingredients

  • all-purpose flour
  • a pinch of salt
  • baking soda
  • 3 cups of white sugar
  • 2 sticks of unsalted butter
  • 6 large eggs
  • vanilla
  • ground mace/ ground mutmeg
  • sour cream

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar, then add the eggs and vanilla.
  3. Add the dry mixture to the wet mixture alternately with sour cream.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Dust the sour cream pound cake with powdered sugar.
